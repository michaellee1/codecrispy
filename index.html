Our Mission

Most students never get the opportunity to learn computer science before college.
We want to introduce programming to elementary and middle school students and show them what it can accomplish in the hope that it will open an opportunity they would never have had.
We also want to give opportunities in places we can't reach; we do this by donating proceeds to trusted charities.
