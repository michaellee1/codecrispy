Our Partners

We donate all of our proceeds to hand-picked organizations that have great goals and mission statements that really represent what Code Crispy is all about

Code.org

Launched in 2013, Code.org is a non-profit dedicated to expanding participation in computer science by making it available in more schools, and increasing participation by women and underrepresented students of color. Their vision is that every student in every school should have the opportunity to learn computer science. Code.org believes that computer science and computer programming should be part of the core curriculum in education, alongside other STEM courses, such as biology, physics, chemistry and algebra.

Scripted.org

Founded in 2012, ScriptEd equips students in under-resourced schools with the fundamental coding skills and professional experiences that together create access to careers in technology. Their courses are taught by experienced software developers on a volunteer basis, ensuring that students have exposure to role models and potential mentors in the field. These students apply their new coding skills in paid summer internships through ScriptEd where they gain the experience and confidence necessary to pursue careers in technology
